The Challenge

A Lagos-based fashion brand was running broad Meta ads with a 1.4× ROAS — barely breaking even. They had strong products and repeat buyers but couldn't scale without burning money.

What We Did

  • Rebuilt the campaign structure around product margin, not just revenue
  • Created a 3-stage funnel: awareness (TikTok UGC), consideration (Meta retargeting), conversion (dynamic product ads)
  • A/B tested 12 creative angles in the first 3 weeks — identified 2 hero creatives
  • Built a post-purchase SMS sequence that recovered 18% of abandoned carts

The Result

ROAS climbed from 1.4× to 7.2× over 60 days. ₦500K in ad spend generated ₦3.6M in tracked revenue. The brand is now scaling to ₦1.5M/month in media spend.

–62%
CAC reduction

The Challenge

A fast-growing food delivery brand in Port Harcourt was acquiring customers at ₦9,800 each — unsustainable given their average order value of ₦4,200. They were growing fast but losing money on every new customer.

What We Did

  • Rebuilt the acquisition funnel with a first-order discount offer that dramatically improved conversion rate
  • Shifted budget from broad awareness to hyper-local radius targeting (3km from delivery zones)
  • Built a WhatsApp re-engagement sequence for lapsed customers — recovered 34% within 30 days
  • Created a loyalty hook in the first order experience to drive repeat purchase

The Result

CAC fell from ₦9,800 to ₦3,700 in 6 weeks. Order frequency increased 2.4×. The brand reached profitability on a per-customer basis for the first time and has since expanded to Abuja.
